Three unbeatable
solutions

With billions yet to get three of the most crucial solutions for human dignity: plant trees, kickstart ecosystems, and support livelihoods.

Plant trees

To combat climate change, conserve biodiversity, and restore CO2s out of the atmosphere. Frontline groups like need your support to plant trees to suck carbon out of the air and restore a rainforest on Earth. They've already planted millions and they are a crucial part of protecting our climate.

Kickstart solar power

Let's face it, our fossil fuel based system needs an upgrade. Instead of relying on CO2, solar can give us all clean cheap energy to burn, we should go all in on renewable technology now. That's why solar is so important: it can bring electricity to millions while keeping fossil fuels in the ground.

Provide clean cookstoves

Nearly half of the world still cooks over open fires, which is bad for the planet and puts human lives. Air pollution from inefficient wood burning stoves contributes to millions of deaths each year. These clean cookstoves, developed with the community, burn efficiently with less smoke, improving health and the planet.
